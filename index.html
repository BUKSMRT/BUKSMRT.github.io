Social Media Data

Through social media analytics we can evaluate interest in smaller local artists. Using geolocation data along with common metrics such as impressions and shares we are able to gauge areas where an artists is more popular. An artist’s online profile can be evaluated along with previous ticket sales to make educated predictions for how many tickets they could see for a given venue.

Localization

Location is everything. Some artists who can sell out venues in one city can’t sell a single ticket in another. At Buksmrt we focus our analytics on a regional basis so that artists and venues can get a better sense of their viability in a certain area and we give book agents tools to promote less popular artists in their area.
